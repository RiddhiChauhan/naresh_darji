I work as a Local Guide (volunteer) on Google Maps. My goal of joining this community as a Local Guide is to provide accurate information to Google map users in our country and promote our tourism and culture.

I am the author of many articles related to Tourism Gujarat and have hosted more than 50 knowledge-sharing meet-ups related to Exploring our Heritage, and healthy contributions on Google Maps and Google CrowdSource in various cities of Gujarat and India.

  • I am a founder of the Gujarat and India Local Guides communities
  • I have added more than 1000 small businesses in Gujarat on Google Maps
  • I have corrected more than 1500 village names in the Gujarati language on Google Maps.
  • I have added more than 10000 roads on Google Maps so that people can get proper navigation.
  • I am also the only community leader in India and one of the influencers of the "Google Crowdsource" community.
  • I am a Platinum Product Expert on Google Maps support forum

I hold the honour of being the first Indian to receive the title of 'Community Builder' and 'Inclusive Mapper' for the Google Maps Guiding Star Awards in 2020 & 2022 along with 50 other Guiding Stars from around the world. My volunteer work supports small businesses, ♿ accessibility, boosting tourism in Gujarat and India, improving the environment, improving regional languages, Gujarati and Hindi in Google Translate and many more

I am also blessed to be named ♿ Accessibility Champion by Google Maps, due to the availability of mapping on Google Maps and the unavailability of wheelchair-accessible access to various businesses, particularly restaurants.

All we do is volunteer work and only for our wonderful country, without expecting any reward from Google. Since we don't work for Google, we are just trying our best to Improve Google maps and promote tourism in India using Google's various platforms (because whenever I write an article about any tourist attraction in India, it gets found on Google

search. can and is visible, mostly at the top) and also for better translation of our regional languages for students who use Google Assistant and Google Translate for their academic projects.

The reason to share all this with you is to create awareness for Google Maps, Local Guides Connect and Google CrowdSource contribution, to help our people and also for travellers who want to know about India's tourism and culture.
